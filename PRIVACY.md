# Privacy Policy

**SplitSet**
Last updated: March 22, 2026

## Overview

SplitSet is a workout companion app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Your privacy is simple: your data stays on your devices.

## Data Collection

SplitSet does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

## Data Stored on Your Devices

SplitSet stores the following data locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch:

- **Workouts you create** — exercise names, set configurations, rep targets, suggested weights, and rest durations. Stored on your iPhone using SwiftData and synced to your Apple Watch via WatchConnectivity (a direct device-to-device connection).
- **Weight logs** — if you enable weight tracking, the weights you log per set are stored on your Apple Watch during the workout session.

This data never leaves your devices.

## HealthKit

SplitSet integrates with Apple HealthKit on Apple Watch to:

- **Read** your heart rate during workouts for real-time display.
- **Write** workout sessions to Apple Health so they appear in your activity history.

HealthKit data is governed by Apple's privacy protections. SplitSet does not access HealthKit data outside of active workout sessions and does not export, share, or transmit any health data.

## Third-Party Services

SplitSet does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no network requests to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

SplitSet does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
